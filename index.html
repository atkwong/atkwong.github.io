About Me

I received my B.Eng. degree from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am currently a M.Phil. student in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at the same university, supervised by Prof. Ling Shi .

My research focuses on the area of multi-robot systems. I have worked on many projects duing my UG studies, including embedded systems, robotics, (analogue and RF) electronics, digital signal and image processing, to name a few.
