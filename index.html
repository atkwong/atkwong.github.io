Avin is currently a Graduate Engineer at Airport Authority Hong Kong (AAHK) working on various electronic technologies and airport systems. He received his B.Eng. and M.Phil. from the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering (ECE), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2019 and 2021, respectively.

His research focused on control and robotics, in particular multi-robot systems (MRS), co-supervised by Prof. Ling Shi and Prof. Winnie Leung. During his UG studies, he worked on many projects in embedded systems, autonomous and robotic systems, circuit design and electronics (analogue & RF), digital signal and image processing, to name a few.
